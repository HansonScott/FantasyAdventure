This is the quest log of your character's adventure.  This log will keep track of the all the in-game plot occurances and tasks that are assigned to you.  Any party members you take on will also enter their entries here, so if you need to read up on their quests, you can find them here.  If you want to make additional notes to yourself regarding any of these topics, use the personal journal section, and add your own new topic with any information you would like.  Copy and paste is active from one sheet to the next, even if you can't see the highlighting taking place on this sheet.
