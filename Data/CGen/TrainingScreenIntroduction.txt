Training your character in specific skills and talents is a fun process to establish what your character is good at doing.  Based on your character's attributes, you are given points to spend on training your character in various skills and feats.  The three main categories listed below separate out different skills and feats by their relative type.  Click on each of them to see a specific description.  The crossover skill points may be used to train your character in any area you choose.  Remember that your character will only progress in levels within the the skills you choose.
