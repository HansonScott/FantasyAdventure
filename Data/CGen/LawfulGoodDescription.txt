Lawful Good, “Crusader”: A lawful good character acts as a good person is expected or required to act. This character combines a commitment to oppose evil with the discipline to fight relentlessly. Lawful good characters tell the truth, keep their word, help those in need, and speak out against injustice. Lawful good characters hate to see the guilty go unpunished. Lawful Good combines honor and compassion.  Lawful Good characters occasionally struggle internally when a situation requires their conscious of good to confront their consious of law.  For example, When a lawful good character finds themselves a part of a corrupt system that is not acting good to its patrons.  Such a onflict can cause a major shift in either personal alignment, or choice of company.
